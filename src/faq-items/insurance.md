question: "Does Hypermobility MD accept insurance?"
category: general
order: 6
---

Hypermobility MD is an out-of-network practice and does not contract with or accept payments from any insurance company. We do not submit claims on your behalf, but a superbill can be provided upon request for possible reimbursement through your out-of-network benefits. Dr. Bluestein does not participate in Medicare or Medicaid.
