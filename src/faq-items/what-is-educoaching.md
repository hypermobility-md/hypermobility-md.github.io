question: "What is EduCoaching?"
category: coaching
order: 1
---

EduCoaching is a unique process developed by Dr. Bluestein that combines personalized information with one-on-one coaching. All sessions are live, interactive video calls with Dr. Bluestein or Rebecca Gluck, PA-C, available worldwide through Bendy Bodies, LLC.

Through EduCoaching, you have the opportunity to meet with an expert in your condition and receive personalized information and guidance to better understand what you are experiencing. Sessions help you understand complex medical information, strengthen self-advocacy skills, and navigate healthcare settings more effectively. The Bendy Bodies team supports you in working with your own medical providers, and provides guidance around health, lifestyle, and emotional aspects of living with these conditions along with practical strategies to improve quality of life.

*Coaching services are for educational and informational purposes only and do not take the place of medical care. Prescriptions and lab work/imaging cannot be ordered in this setting.*
