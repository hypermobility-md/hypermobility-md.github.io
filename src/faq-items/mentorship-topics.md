**Clinical mentorship topics:**

- Diagnosing and managing EDS and HSD
- Navigating complex comorbidities (POTS, MCAS, CCI, etc.)
- Improving patient care in under-recognized conditions

**Business mentorship topics:**

- Building a niche, mission-driven practice
- Ethical entrepreneurship and sustainable growth
- Finding balance in a high-demand field

Each session is tailored to your needs. You can bring a challenging patient case, questions about treatment planning, help interpreting symptoms or lab results, concerns about scope of practice, or guidance on growing your practice.
