We offer three session options to fit different needs:

**Gold Package** (Least Complex)

- Designed for a single, specific, limited health concern
- Typically 90 minutes for the first appointment and 25 minutes for follow-up

**Platinum Package** (Moderately Complex)

- Designed for patients who need a comprehensive review or have multiple related concerns
- Typically 150 minutes for the first appointment and 50 minutes for follow-up

**Diamond Package** (Most Complex)

- Comprehensive evaluations, extensive assessments, or complex medical histories
- Recommended for most patients with EDS, HSD, POTS, and MCAS
- Typically 210 minutes for the first appointment and 90 minutes for follow-up

Full pricing and package details are in our [pricing and packages document](/assets/HMD_Pricing.pdf). More information is also available on the [patient portal](https://hypermobilitymd.md-hq.com/) and in our [office policies and procedures](/assets/HMD_Policies_and_Procedures.pdf).
