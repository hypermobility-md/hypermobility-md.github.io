question: "How much do new patient medical appointments cost?"
category: medical
order: 5
---

New patient appointments are offered as one of three packages, selected to match the complexity of your medical history. Each package includes one follow-up visit, which must be scheduled within 2 months of the initial appointment.

- **Gold** — single, discrete medical issue. 90-minute initial visit, 25-minute follow-up. **$1,800**
- **Platinum** — 2–3 straightforward medical issues. 150-minute initial visit, 50-minute follow-up. **$2,500**
- **Diamond** — complex medical issue(s). 210-minute initial visit, 90-minute follow-up. **$3,000**

The first appointment must be in person in Wisconsin or Colorado. Full details are in our [pricing and packages document](/assets/HMD_Pricing.pdf).
