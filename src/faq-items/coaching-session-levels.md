category: coaching
order: 3

**Bronze** (20 minutes): Personalized one-on-one coaching focused on a single issue.

**Silver** (45 minutes)

- Detailed review of 2–3 issues
- Review of up to 10 pages of medical records
- Discount code for supplements

**Gold** (100 minutes)

- Review of complex, multi-system problems
- Review of up to 30 pages of medical records
- Complimentary follow-up call (up to 15 minutes) within 30 days
- Custom handouts designed by Dr. Bluestein

**Platinum** (100 minutes)

- Peer-to-peer consultation with your primary care provider (up to 15 minutes)
- Access to Dr. Bluestein's trusted resources
