---
question: "How much do follow-up visits cost for existing patients?"
category: medical
order: 6
---

- **25 minutes**: for very limited issues, **$350**
- **50 minutes**: standard follow-up, **$600**
- **105 minutes**: recommended for multiple or complex topics, **$900**

Most follow-ups are scheduled every 1–3 months. To remain an active patient, you must be seen in person at least once every 12 months.
