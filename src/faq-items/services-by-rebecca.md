Rebecca Gluck, PA-C offers EduCoaching sessions through Bendy Bodies, LLC. She brings specialized experience in Ehlers-Danlos syndromes, hypermobility spectrum disorders, and related conditions, with a background in genetics and ongoing involvement with the Ehlers-Danlos Society.

Sessions with Rebecca provide a more accessible option for expert education and coaching support for individuals navigating complex health challenges. All sessions are live, interactive video calls available worldwide.
