Dr. Bluestein consults on cases involving Ehlers-Danlos Syndromes, Hypermobility Spectrum Disorders, Mast Cell Activation Syndrome (MCAS), Postural Orthostatic Tachycardia Syndrome (POTS), and related conditions. These are exactly the diagnoses most likely to be misdiagnosed, dismissed, or poorly managed by generalist clinicians, and they are her exclusive area of clinical practice.

She consults on a wide range of medical-legal matters, including personal injury cases, disability cases, divorce and family law matters, complex medical causation questions, delayed or missed diagnoses, perioperative complications, chronic pain, dysautonomia, mast cell disorders, and cases involving medically complex patients with connective tissue disorders.
