Most people with hypermobility have hypermobile EDS (hEDS) or a hypermobility spectrum disorder (HSD). But certain features &mdash; "red flags" &mdash; can suggest a *genetically-defined* type of EDS or another heritable disorder of connective tissue (HDCT) that may need specific testing and monitoring.

The table below, organized by body system, lists red-flag features and the conditions each may point toward. It is a tool for recognizing patterns and preparing for appointments &mdash; **not** a diagnostic checklist. Always discuss your history with a knowledgeable clinician.

<p class="resource-source-note">Adapted from Tables 2&ndash;4 in Francomano CA, Hakim AJ, Henderson LGS &amp; Henderson FC. <em>Symptomatic</em> (Elsevier, 2024).</p>
