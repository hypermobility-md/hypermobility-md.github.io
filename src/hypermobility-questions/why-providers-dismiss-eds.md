Unfortunately, many healthcare providers still receive limited education about EDS and HSD during their training. Some may not recognize the wide range of symptoms these conditions can cause, or they may underestimate their impact on daily life. This is one of the reasons that patient education and self-advocacy are so important — and why resources like the [Bendy Bodies Podcast](/podcast/) and organizations like The Ehlers-Danlos Society exist to help bridge the knowledge gap.
