Yes. While a hypermobility disorder may require adjustments, many people with these conditions live active, fulfilling lives. Many go on to have successful careers, families, and hobbies. The key is finding the right management strategies, building a supportive care team, and learning to listen to your body. Some symptoms like POTS may also improve with time and appropriate treatment.
