Navigating healthcare with EDS or HSD can be challenging, but education and self-advocacy are powerful tools. Here are some key strategies:

- **Educate yourself**: Understanding your condition empowers you to have more productive conversations with your healthcare team
- **Document your medical history**: Keep organized records, including a timeline of symptoms, diagnoses, and treatments
- **Advocate for yourself**: If a provider is unfamiliar with your condition, be prepared to share educational resources and ask for referrals
- **Diversify your care team**: Consider specialists such as rheumatologists, geneticists, PM&R physicians, pain medicine specialists, and physical therapists experienced with hypermobility
