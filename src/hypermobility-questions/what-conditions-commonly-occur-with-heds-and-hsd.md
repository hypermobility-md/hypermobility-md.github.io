Common co-occurring conditions include dysautonomia such as postural orthostatic tachycardia syndrome, gastrointestinal motility disorders, migraine, temporomandibular joint dysfunction, anxiety or sensory sensitivity, and mast cell activation–type symptoms. Not every person has all comorbidities, but they are more common in hypermobility disorders than in the general population.
