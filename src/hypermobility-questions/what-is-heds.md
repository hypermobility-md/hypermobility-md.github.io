**Hypermobile Ehlers-Danlos syndrome (hEDS)** is an inherited connective tissue condition characterized by generalized joint hypermobility, joint instability, and a range of symptoms that can affect multiple body systems, including pain, fatigue, and autonomic or gastrointestinal dysfunction.
