Hypermobility can affect much more than joints. Common symptoms that come along with hypermobility include:

- Joint pain and instability
- Frequent sprains, strains, joint dislocations, or subluxations (partial dislocations)
- Fatigue
- Headaches and migraines
- Dizziness and POTS symptoms (postural orthostatic tachycardia syndrome)
- Gastrointestinal issues
- Easy bruising
- Chronic pain
- Altered proprioception and coordination
