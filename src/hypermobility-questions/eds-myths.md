Some of the most common myths include:

- **"It's just flexible joints"** — EDS and HSD affect connective tissue throughout the entire body, not just the joints
- **"It's benign"** — These conditions can cause significant pain, fatigue, and dysfunction across multiple body systems
- **"You'll grow out of it"** — While some symptoms may change over time, these are lifelong conditions
- **"If you can't do party tricks, you don't have it"** — Many people with hypermobility disorders lose flexibility over time or may never have been dramatically flexible
