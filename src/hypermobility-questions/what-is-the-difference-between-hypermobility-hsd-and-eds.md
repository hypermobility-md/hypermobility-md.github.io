Joint hypermobility describes increased joint range of motion. Hypermobility Spectrum Disorder (HSD) is diagnosed when hypermobility causes symptoms that are not explained by another condition. Hypermobile Ehlers-Danlos syndrome (hEDS) is a specific connective tissue disorder with defined diagnostic criteria. Both HSD and hEDS can significantly impact health and function.
