Many people are diagnosed late because hypermobility is often dismissed as “just being flexible.” Symptoms such as pain, fatigue, and autonomic issues may be misattributed to anxiety, deconditioning, or unrelated conditions. Awareness of hypermobility-related disorders has increased only in recent years, contributing to delayed recognition.
