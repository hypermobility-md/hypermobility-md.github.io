Hypermobility disorders can affect multiple body systems. In addition to joint pain and instability, people may experience fatigue, brain fog, headaches or migraines, dizziness, gastrointestinal symptoms such as bloating or reflux, and autonomic symptoms such as rapid heart rate or temperature regulation issues. Symptoms often fluctuate and may worsen with stress, illness, or overexertion.
