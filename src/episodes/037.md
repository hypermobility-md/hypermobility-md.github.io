[17:25] So what sort of advice would you offer people to help prepare for those medical appointments?

[17:34] **Dr. Linda Bluestein:** Preparation is so extremely important. I recommend that people first start out by thinking about their goals. What is it that they want to get out of this next appointment? And try not to bring previous appointments into that next appointment with you. [18:53] I think that can be very helpful, especially with teenagers. I see a lot of teenage patients — females in particular — and it's hard enough going through those teenage years without having chronic illness, but add chronic illness on top of it.
