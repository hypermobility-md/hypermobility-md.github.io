People who are hypermobile may have issues — thinking of nearby regions — with hip instability, femoroacetabular impingement, or other joint issues within the hip that need some stability and strength. That might be driving people to do too much without realizing it's part of their behavioral patterning. Trying to find a balance with that is important.
