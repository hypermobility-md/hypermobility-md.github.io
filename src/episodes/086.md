[29:36] **Amanda Cassil, PhD:** I can talk about grief all day long. We often think about grief — or a lot of us are raised thinking about grief — related to death. [30:25] We have to acknowledge that it's not just somebody lost something concrete and tangible, but even our understanding of who we are, who we thought we could become — that starts to shift.
