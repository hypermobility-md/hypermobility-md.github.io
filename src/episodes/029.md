[19:25] The second part is in every individual shot, and this is why when people work with me, we're not working for 20 or 30 or 40 images in a session. [20:09] By that I mean, for example, an arabesque: your fingers would be in line with your shoulder naturally, but oftentimes for shooting we have to bring the arm across and in front of the nose a little bit. [21:29] But the third part — and this is the area I really love — is that by working through that system, you're really able to have the dancers feel confident and free to express themselves.
