[06:11] **Dr. Linda Bluestein:** That makes sense, and that's such an important theme for dancers with hypermobility — that slow and low burn approach rather than hard and fast and aggressive. [17:26] **Beckanne Sisk:** I have learned that warming up before I stretch is a big thing for me. For instance, I'll do a plank on my forearms, a regular plank to kind of start the day. [17:59] My stretching isn't necessarily — and I'm not saying this is the right thing, everyone is different — but I feel like as a young dancer I maybe overstretched too much, and sometimes it just made me hurt throughout the day. [19:14] **Beckanne Sisk:** Yeah, and I have never done an over-split and then been like, "Oh look, I can hold my leg up here now." It's more like when I do workouts to strengthen my muscles, then I can hold my leg up. It's not necessarily about stretching.
