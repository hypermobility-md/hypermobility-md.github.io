[17:37] It didn't involve a full hypermobility assessment; it just involved consideration of what's called generalized joint hypermobility. [19:51] We had 109 patients with neurodevelopmental conditions. In the paper, we explain why we're using the word "neurodivergence" — we wanted to take a non-deficit-based approach. [21:50] So, in line with non-ableist language, we think about how we describe these things in the scientific literature. [30:52] We addressed that through something called a mediation analysis, which is where you have a number of different variables that are all related and you want to see if the relationship between two things — in this case, neurodivergence and physical health symptoms — is in fact being exerted by a different variable. [31:59] It was a relatively small sample — 109 neurodivergent individuals.
