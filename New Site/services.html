Medical Services

Dr. Bluestein uses integrative medicine to identify the source of your pain and the biochemical imbalances that may be making it worse. She utilizes personalized medicine to treat the root cause — not just the symptoms.

Using cutting-edge testing and the latest research, Dr. Bluestein provides modern treatments including supplements, lifestyle changes, and medications to restore your health in the best way possible.

Note: Dr. Bluestein works as an integrative pain medicine consultant in conjunction with your current health care team. All patients must have a primary care physician for emergencies and routine care.

Education & Coaching

Bendy Bodies offers EduCoaching — a unique process combining personalized information with one-on-one coaching with Dr. Linda Bluestein, MD and Rebecca Gluck, PA-C.

Joint hypermobility and related conditions are often missed by the standard healthcare system. Our EduCoaching program enables individuals worldwide to benefit from the expertise of the Bendy Bodies team from the comfort of your own home.

Rebecca Gluck, PA-C brings a unique blend of clinical expertise and a background in leading global education and support programs, enabling her to inform, validate, and support clients as they navigate life with hypermobility.

Note: EduCoaching sessions are one-on-one client sessions. These are NOT medical appointments and are not a replacement for your own medical team.
